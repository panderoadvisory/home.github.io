About

Pandero Advisory

Pandero Advisory is a specialist advisory firm focused on private equity and distressed investments across number of sectors including energy and renewables, infrastructure, and private credit,

Our Strategy

Our strategy is to help investment firms to allocate funds in European businesses with strong, highly motivated management teams and to provide the support necessary to create exceptional returns. We target businesses that can scale quickly, often from a small base.

Our Approach

We assist investment management teams to unlock value of businesses they invest in and to drive performance on a day-to-day basis. We are able to help enhance business performance by leveraging our experience of diverse spectrum of asset classes, and by applying novel approaches to optimize processes.

Services

What We Do For You?

We adopt a highly analytical and granular approach towards the valuation and due diligence of potential investments, which is facilitated by the use of proprietary tools to price assets and unlock hidden value within diverse business processes.

Fund Launch Support

We help you to define and understand your target audience. We support your team when navigating the landscape of your prospective partners.

Process Optimization

We are experts in identifying bottlenecks of investment or industrial processes. We help you to design optimal production strategy that will maximize your asset performance and hence your return.

Strategy Development

We are developing bespoke strategies for liquid markets. Our strategies help you to obtain exposure to your desired asset classes and tailor the risk return profile.
